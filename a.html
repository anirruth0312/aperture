About

It all started in the year 2020. The covid 19 pandemic. I used to sit up at my terrace every evening looking at the birds flying past my head. I always wondered what they looked like up close while they were flying... That's when I decided to make use of dad's DSLR camera and my neighbour is a part time Photographer so I decided to approach him as he knew quite a bit about Photography and how the camera worked... He taught me the basics of Photography. And I first practiced on still objects like plants household items just to get a hang of the camera and surprisingly it did not take me much time. That's when I thought why not go up to the terrace and give it a go...and surprisingly using the right settings I was able to make it work...I then decided to step up my game and I took up an online course on Photography which was for about 2 weeks which really did help me and from then on I have been exploring the world through my lens
